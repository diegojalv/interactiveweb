Moving to the US

At the age of 15, I took a courageous step and moved to the United States with a dream for a better life and the pursuit of education. This journey was not an easy one, but it shaped who I am today.

Leaving my parents behind, I arrived in a new country with determination and hope.

During my sophomore year in college, life presented me with another unexpected but joyous challenge. I became a father to a wonderful daughter. Balancing academics, soccer, and fatherhood was tough, but it fueled my motivation to overcome every adversity.

Through hard work, perseverance, and the support of those around me, I triumphed over challenges that seemed insurmountable. Each obstacle became a stepping stone toward success, and I learned valuable lessons about resilience and determination.
